### Pokemon App (Decided this APP )

- ### Category : Entertainment

- ### Pokemon app that allows users to input pokemon name and detailed descriptions and image of pokemon will be presented. App would be pure entertainment. 
- ### Feature List : Details of pokemon , Images of pokemon, Pokedex UI , Images , and Generation , etc.. 


Would have to find Reliable API for pokemon information. 

Also would want the UI to be user friendly and encourage new Users. 

Could maybe have a pokemon of the day feature where one random pokemon is on the home screen with all its information. 

Would use fire base to login and save favorite pokemon. 

- ### Mobile: App is pretty unique do not see many other similar apps. Easy to use very accesbile on the phone.  

- ### Category : Entertainment

- ### Story: Story is compelling with out reach to hardcore pokemon fans or even competitions. 

- ### Market: Market isn't generally high but directly focused on the niche fans of pokemon

- ### Habit : App isn't habit inducing intended for quick use information seeking pokemon fans. 

- ### Scope: Don't think the app will be TOO challenging to create.
